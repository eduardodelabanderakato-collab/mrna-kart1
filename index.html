SCIENCE FOCUS
Protein synthesis turns DNA instructions into a protein.
In transcription, RNA polymerase reads DNA in the nucleus and builds messenger RNA using A, U, G, and C. In translation, the ribosome reads mRNA codons in the cytoplasm while tRNA anticodons bring amino acids to build a protein.
